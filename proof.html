Rates:


  • Regular turnaround (3-4 days):   50 cents/page (add 10 cents for court)
  • Rush turnaround (24-72 hours):   55 cents/page (add 10 cents for court)
  • Same day turnaround (less than 24 hours):   60 cents/page (add 10 cents for court)
  • Daily turnaround (immediate and late night):   75 cents/page (add 10 cents for court)

(For transcripts sent to me in the middle of the night, the clock starts ticking at 8:00 a.m.)
(Rates are based on up to 25 lines per page.)

Method of Delivery:


Please email transcripts in PDF format to: Nina.Proofreading@gmail.com. Transcripts are proofread on an iPad using iAnnotate software. Markings will be made directly on the pages. A marked-up PDF file will be emailed back. Most markings are in red, but occasionally you may see yellow highlighting or stamps such as smiley faces, arrows, checkmarks, etc. Only pages with corrections on them are emailed back.

Nina Dudziak has been a freelance court reporter since 1994. She was trained as an in-house proofreader by a large Chicago agency in 1997 and has been proofreading for court reporters since then. She owned a court reporting agency (Nina Dudziak Court Reporters) until 2008 when it was sold to a national agency. She then worked as a consultant and was in charge of quality control for five years. Currently she proofreads for over 20 reporters, is an in-house proofreader for a large agency, and mentors new reporters and students.
